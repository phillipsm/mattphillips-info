hovermarks.org

About

Hovermarks help you annotate a bookshelf. They are a lightweight approach to highlighting items on a shelf and can breath fresh air into a stale section of your library's stacks.

Hovermarks are papercraft. They are printed patterns that are folded and placed in an item just like a regular bookmark.

Why

Bookshelves and library stacks should be interactive. They should invite curration, and not only from librarians. Hovermarks encourage this playfulness by being easy to create and cheap enough to be disposable.

The ephemerality of the Hovermark is its strength. It's like street art. An empty wall is a canvas. A member of the community decorates it and when it fades away, it's ready for someone else to impart it with their vision.
